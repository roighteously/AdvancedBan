JavaDocs for AdvancedBan.

Quick overview:

Check out AdvancedBan on GitHub: https://github.com/DevLeoko/AdvancedBan
Or on Spigot: https://www.spigotmc.org/resources/advancedban.8695/

If you want to get a players history or active punishments (and some more), see: {@link me.leoko.advancedban.manager.PunishmentManager}.
If you need to create a new Punishment, see: {@link me.leoko.advancedban.utils.Punishment#create(String,String,String,String,PunishmentType,Long,String,boolean) Punishment.create(...)}
And to listen for new punishments or punishments being revoked, see:
- me.leoko.advancedban.bukkit.event (for Bukkit)
- me.leoko.advancedban.bungee.event (for Bungeecord)

@see me.leoko.advancedban.bukkit.event @see me.leoko.advancedban.bungee.event
